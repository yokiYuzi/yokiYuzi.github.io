PROJECT EXPERIENCE

Self-Supervised Neural Signal Representation Learning 10/2025-Current

  • Designed a hypergraph-guided self-supervised framework for motor-imagery EEG representation learning on BCICIV-2a using channel–frequency–time EEG tokens.
  • Developed semantic hyperedge masking and JEPA-style latent prediction to model interpretable spatial–spectral–temporal dependencies across sensorimotor regions, μ/β rhythms, and MI stages.
  • Built downstream MI classification and interpretability pipelines using edge-level probing, path contribution analysis, and counterfactual hyperedge ablation to validate learned EEG representations.

Fetal ECG Reconstruction & Arrhythmia Detection 03/2025-09/2025

  • Developed FetalHealthNet (FHNet) for fetal ECG reconstruction and arrhythmia detection from noisy multi-lead abdominal ECG.
  • Built graph-aware spatiotemporal attention and multi-scale temporal modules to model inter-lead dynamics and fetal QRS morphology.
  • Outperformed prior best models across public datasets, improving reconstruction R² by 8%, reducing RMSE by 45%, and increasing arrhythmia detection F1 by 5%.

Federated Learning Expertise in Minimax Problem Scenarios 08/2024-08/2025

  • Skilled in the implementation and optimization of both centralized and decentralized federated learning frameworks.
  • Utilizes advanced mathematical derivations to analyze the convergence properties of optimization algorithms, including both strongly concave and non-convex functions, effectively approximating their upper convergence bounds.
  • Tackles critical challenges in federated learning, specifically addressing the inversion of gradient information to enhance privacy protection measures within distributed learning environments.
